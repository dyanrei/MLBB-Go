Marksman

Marksman is a type of role that has a high attack/damage and critical and distance. When using this hero, be aware of its weakness, because this type of hero usually has low health and durability.

Assassin

Assassin is a type of role that has high burst damage, specializing in their attacks. This role has low durability and health.

Fighter

Fighters are close range combatants that possess a mix of offensive and defensive capabilities. While they don't have as much utility as a tank or as much damage as an assassin.

Mage

Mage is a type of role that has high magic power and high CD in skills, specializing in dealing with magic damage with their skills. This role usually has low durability and health. Most heroes in this role have high burst damage.

Tank

A tank is a type of role that is durable, has high HP and high defense while having a very low attack. They are naturallly bulky, higher natural HP, Magic Resistance, and Armor.

Support

Support is a type of role that has low attack damages and commonly relies on magic damage. When using this hero, be knowledgeable that it has low durability, making them look squishy.
